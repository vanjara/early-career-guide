# Visa / OPT Resources

## Tools

| Tool | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **H1Bdata.info** | Search H1B filing history by company — use before every application |
| **myvisajobs.com** | Job board filtered by H1B sponsoring employers |
| **USCIS.gov** | Primary source for OPT, STEM extension, H1B rules — always current |
| **Murthy Law Firm** (murthy.com) | Best free guides on F-1, OPT, H1B mechanics |
| **trackitt.com** | H1B and visa processing time tracker — community-reported |

## Important: paid "H1B guides"

Most paid H1B guides and courses are outdated or generic. The free resources above — USCIS.gov, Murthy Law Firm, and the Reddit communities — are more accurate and more current.

For complex individual situations (RFE, status violations, layoff on visa), talk to an actual immigration attorney. Reddit is for general guidance, not legal advice.

---

## Your DSO (Designated School Official)

Your university's international student office is your most important resource. They:
- Process your OPT application
- Authorize CPT
- Handle STEM extension paperwork
- Can advocate for you with employers in some situations

Build a relationship with them before you need them urgently. Know their office hours, processing timelines, and what documentation they need.
